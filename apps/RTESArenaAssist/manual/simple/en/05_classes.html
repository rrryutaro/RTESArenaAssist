Character Classes (All 18)

Thief Classes — Primary Stats: Agility (AGI) & Intelligence (INT)

All thief classes can pick locks, pick pockets, and steal. High Speed is also desirable.

Thief

Quick and agile. Useful in combat for surprise, scouting, and critical hits. Fastest class to gain experience levels.

★ Gains critical hit chance (3× damage) per level
WeaponsDagger, Shortsword, Broadsword, Saber, War Axe, Short Bow
ArmorLeather only
ShieldBuckler only
Starting Health25 + d10

Burglar

Expert at picking locks and infiltrating restricted areas. Cannot use shields.

★ Critical hit chance per level (lower than Thief)
WeaponsDagger, Short Sword, Tanto, Short Bow
ArmorLeather only
ShieldNone
Starting Health25 + d8

Assassin

Dedicated to killing. Highest critical hit chance of any class. Can use any weapon but restricted to leather armor and no shield.

★ Highest critical hit chance per level of any class
WeaponsAny
ArmorLeather only
ShieldNone
Starting Health25 + d12

Rogue

A thief trained in arms and armor. Comparable in combat to warriors while retaining pick lock/pocket abilities.

★ Slight critical hit chance per level
WeaponsAny
ArmorLeather or Chain only
ShieldAny except Tower
Starting Health25 + d14

Acrobat

Supernaturally agile — fastest character on foot. Reduces enemy base hit chance passively.

★ Reduces opponent's base hit chance / Critical hit chance per level
WeaponsDagger, Shortsword, Broadsword, Tanto, Shortbow
ArmorLeather only
ShieldNone
Starting Health25 + d8

Bard

"Jack of all trades." Handles critical strikes, weapons, thieving, and magic. Spell Points = INT.

WeaponsDagger, Shortsword, Broadsword, Saber, Mace, War Axe, Shortbow
ArmorLeather or Chain only
ShieldAny except Tower Shield
Starting Health25 + d10
Warrior Classes — Primary Stats: Strength (STR) & Endurance (END)

Cannot cast spells unless otherwise noted. Agility also important for Armor Rating.

Warrior

The backbone of Tamriel. Can use any weapon, armor, or shield. Second fastest to gain levels. Only Warriors, Knights, and Rangers may wear enchanted plate armor.

WeaponsAny
ArmorAny
ShieldAny
Starting Health25 + d20

Knight

Noble fighter. Automatically repairs all weapons and armor in inventory.

★ Immune to paralyzation (spells and poison) / Auto-repairs weapons and armor
WeaponsAny
ArmorNot Leather
ShieldAny
Starting Health25 + d18

Ranger

Tracker and survivalist. Automatically reduces travel time between cities. Deals bonus damage equal to level.

★ Reduced travel time between cities / Bonus damage = level
WeaponsAny
ArmorAny
ShieldAny except Tower
Starting Health25 + d18

Archer

Marksman specialized in missile weapons. Critical hit chance with bows per level. Cannot use shields.

★ Critical hit chance with missile weapons per level
WeaponsAny
ArmorLeather or Chain only
ShieldNone
Starting Health25 + d16

Monk

Mind and body honed into a killing machine. No armor or shield. Enemy hit chance decreases each level. Spell saves yield no damage.

★ Reduces enemy hit chance per level / Successful spell save = zero damage / Critical hit (melee only)
WeaponsAny
ArmorNone
ShieldNone
Starting Health25 + d14

Barbarian

Survivor of harsh lands. Highest starting health of all classes. Heals bonus health based on Endurance.

★ Immune to poison / Bonus health restored per rest based on END
WeaponsAny
ArmorUp to Chain
ShieldAny
Starting Health25 + d30 (highest of all classes)
Mage Classes — Primary Stats: Intelligence (INT) & Willpower (WIL)

Mage

Born for magic. Spell Points = INT×2. Depends entirely on spells for offense and defense. No armor or shield.

WeaponsDagger, Staff
ArmorNone
ShieldBuckler only
Starting Health25 + d6

Sorcerer

A magical "battery." Cannot generate spell points internally; instead absorbs them from spells targeted at them (up to INT×3). When fully charged, potentially the most powerful of all mage classes.

★ Absorbs targeted spells → no damage + gains spell points / Cannot absorb own spells / No natural SP regeneration
WeaponsAny
ArmorUp to Chain
ShieldNone
Starting Health25 + d6

Healer

Defensive magic specialist. Spell Points = INT×1.75. Defensive spells cost less in Spellmaker. Increased base healing rate.

WeaponsDagger, Staff, Mace, Flail
ArmorLeather or Chain only
ShieldUp to Round
Starting Health25 + d8

Battle Mage

Offensive spell specialist. Spell Points = INT×1.75. Offensive spells cost less in Spellmaker.

WeaponsAny
ArmorLeather only
ShieldUp to Round
Starting Health25 + d10

Spellsword

Can cast spells while wearing armor. Versatile warrior-mage. Spell Points = INT×1.5.

WeaponsAny
ArmorLeather or Chain only
ShieldAny except Tower Shield
Starting Health25 + d12

Nightblade

Mage of stealth and infiltration. Lock-picking comparable to Rogues. Critical hit chance per level. Spell Points = INT×1.5.

★ Critical hit chance per level
WeaponsDagger, Staff, Short Bow, Short Sword, Saber
ArmorLeather only
ShieldBuckler only
Starting Health25 + d8
