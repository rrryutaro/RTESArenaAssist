Character Creation

Game Start Options

  1. Start New Game — Begin a fresh game; quests and NPCs are initialized.
  2. Load Saved Game — Resume from a previously saved game.
  3. Exit — Return to DOS.

Two Ways to Create Your Character

  1. Generate
    Answer 10 questions to determine which class best fits your personality. The suggested class is only a guideline — you may choose any class regardless of your answers.
  2. Select
    Choose directly from a list of all 18 character classes.

Homeland & Name

After choosing a class, select your homeland (province) and enter your character's name. Your province determines your race.

Stat Distribution

All stats range from 1 to 100, with 50 being average. Your chosen race provides a base stat range. Distribute Bonus Points among stats to personalize your character. Focus on the stats most important for your chosen class.

Example: A STR of 72 means your character is stronger than 71% of the Empire's population.

Choosing a Face

After distributing stats, choose your character's appearance. Left-click to cycle through available faces. Click Done when finished — your adventure begins.
