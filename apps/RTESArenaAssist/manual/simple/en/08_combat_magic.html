Combat & Magic

Combat Basics

Arena uses real-time combat. Click to attack. Whether you hit, and how much damage you deal, depends on your stats, your equipment, and the enemy's Armor Rating (AR).

Damage

Damage = weapon base value + STR modifier + race bonus. The target's Armor Rating (AR) reduces incoming damage — the lower the AR, the better the protection.

Critical Hit

Critical Hit = 3× normal weapon damage.
Thief-class characters gain a per-level chance to score one. Assassins have the highest chance. Rogues, Burglars, Bards, Acrobats, Monks, Archers (with bows), and Nightblades also have this ability.

Saving Throw

When targeted by a spell or special effect, a successful saving throw reduces or negates the effect. WIL (Willpower) governs success chance.

Magic System

Spells are selected from your Spellbook (character sheet Page 2). Each spell costs Spell Points (SP). When SP reaches 0, no spells can be cast. SP is determined by INT × a class-specific multiplier.

Spellmaker

Available at the Mages Guild. Create custom spells by combining effects, range, duration, and magnitude. Healers get discounts on defensive effects; Battle Mages get discounts on offensive effects.

Experience & Leveling

Earn experience by defeating enemies and completing quests. Leveling up increases stats and health (and SP for casters).

Level-up speed (fastest first): Thief → Warrior → Warrior subclasses → Thief subclasses → Mage → Mage subclasses
