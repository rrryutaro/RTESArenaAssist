Character Sheet — Page 2 (Equipment)

ColorMeaning
YellowCurrently equipped item
TanEquippable item
RedCannot be equipped
CyanUnidentified magical item
Bright cyanUnidentified magical item (equipped)

Left-click to equip an item. Right-click for item info. Scroll arrows navigate up to 40 items.

Drop: Drop the highlighted item. Spellbook: Show spells. Exit: Return to Page 1.
