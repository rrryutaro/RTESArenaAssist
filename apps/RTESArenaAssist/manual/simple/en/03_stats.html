Character Stats

All stats range from 1–100. 50 is average — 50% of the Empire's population is better than you at that stat, and 50% are not.

The 8 Primary Stats

STR — Strength

Governs how much damage you do in combat. Affects maximum carry weight (Max Kilos), fatigue, and jump distance. Essential for warrior classes.

INT — Intelligence

Determines total spell points for spellcasters. Affects negotiation, price haggling, and lock-picking success. Essential for mage and thief classes.

WIL — Willpower

Governs resistance to spells (Magic Defense). Also helps with negotiations. Vital for any character who wants spell protection.

AGI — Agility

Directly affects melee hit chance (To Hit), chance to be hit (To Def), and chance to dodge targeted spells. Also governs stealing and lock-picking. Essential for thief classes.

SPD — Speed

Controls movement rate, turning speed, and missile weapon fire rate. Can make the difference between life and death. Important for all classes.

END — Endurance

Governs hit point totals, healing rate, and fatigue. Also affects resistance to poisons and drugs. Essential for warrior classes.

PER — Personality

Determines Charisma modifier. Directly affects ability to negotiate, get better prices, and gather information. Important for social interactions.

LUC — Luck

A powerful modifier to every action — attacking, defending, stealing, anything. Average is 50; higher is better. Affects all characters.
