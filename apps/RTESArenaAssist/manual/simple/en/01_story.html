Story & Quest Overview

Key Characters

Talin — The player character. An adventurer imprisoned in the sewers of the Imperial City.

Ria Silmane — Former apprentice to Jagar Tharn. Killed by Tharn, she persists as a spirit to guide the player through dreams.

Jagar Tharn — The Imperial Battle Mage of Tamriel. The main villain who imprisoned the true Emperor and took his place.

Uriel Septim VII — The true Emperor, imprisoned in a pocket dimension where time passes much more slowly.

Background

Jagar Tharn imprisoned Emperor Uriel Septim VII in an alternate dimension where time flows far more slowly than in Tamriel. Since the Emperor is not dead, the Amulet of Kings will not alert the Elder Council for generations. Tharn uses this time to rule the Empire in disguise.

The Opening

The player character Talin awakens in a dungeon cell in the Imperial City sewers. Ria Silmane's spirit appears and reveals the truth. She links her mind to Talin's so she can reach him only through dreams — to maintain this link, the player must rest periodically.

"You are entering a dangerous arena, my friend. One in which the players are beings beyond your mortal comprehension. I see in your destiny the ability to transcend." — Ria Silmane

The Main Quest

Objective: Recover the eight pieces of the Staff of Chaos to open the gateway to the dimension where the Emperor is imprisoned.

The eight pieces of the Staff of Chaos are scattered across dungeons throughout Tamriel. Ria reveals each location in dreams once the player has gained sufficient experience.

Tips

Build up your character in side dungeons (crypts, ruins, lairs) before attempting the main quest pieces. Always read dream messages and letters — they contain critical quest information. The Arena world is deadly to the unprepared; patience is essential.
