Tavern / Inn

The most important source of information in any city. Talk to NPCs to hear rumors and pick up quests. Rent a room to rest — this restores Health and Fatigue, and is the only way Ria Silmane can appear in your dreams.

Dreams are the key to main quest progression. Ria will reveal the locations of the Staff of Chaos pieces only when you have gained sufficient experience — and only through dreams.

Temple

Removes status ailments for gold.

ServiceEffect
Cure DiseaseRemoves the Diseased condition
Cure PoisonRemoves the Poisoned condition
Remove CurseRemoves the Cursed condition
BlessGrants a temporary stat bonus

Mages Guild

Essential for spellcasting classes. Purchase spells to add to your spellbook or create custom spells in the Spellmaker. Offers paid city-to-city teleportation and item identification.

Fighters Guild

Offers a steady stream of short-term quests (kill contracts, escort missions, retrieval jobs). Completing them yields gold and experience — one of the fastest ways to level up early in the game.

Equipment Store

Buy and sell weapons and armor. Repair, identification, and enchantment services also available. Stock varies by location.

Automap

Press Tab to open the automap in dungeons and cities. It records explored areas and shows your current position and facing direction. Use it to avoid getting lost in deep dungeons.
