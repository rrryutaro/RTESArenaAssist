Races

Nord — Province of Skyrim

Tall, fair-skinned people who are strong, willful, and hardy. Their arctic upbringing has inured them to cold.

⚔ Special: Half damage from Cold-based attacks; no damage on a successful saving throw. Excel in blade and shield arts.

Khajiit — Province of Elsweyr

Fair-skinned, extremely hardy, intelligent, and agile. Said to descend from an intelligent feline race.

⚔ Special: Expert climbers — scale walls faster than any other race. Adept at thieving and sleight of hand.

Redguard — Province of Hammerfell

Stocky, powerful, and innately proficient with weapons. Renowned as the most naturally skilled weapon users in Tamriel.

⚔ Special: Receive (level ÷ 3) as a bonus to hit and damage with any melee weapon (not bows). Excel in blade and shield arts.

Breton — Province of High Rock

Tall, dark-haired, highly intelligent and willful. Magic seems to suffuse their very being.

⚔ Special: Half damage from Magic-based attacks; no damage on a successful saving throw. Excel in arcane arts.

Argonian — Province of Black Marsh

A highly evolved reptilian race, at home in marsh environments. Known for intelligence, agility, and speed.

⚔ Special: Swim faster than any other race; rarely drown. Adept in arcane arts and thievery.

Wood Elf — Province of Valenwood

People of the forests, extremely agile and quick. Well suited to any class, with nimbleness especially useful for theft.

⚔ Special: Receive (level ÷ 3) as a bonus to hit and damage with any bow weapon.

High Elf — Province of Summurset Isle

Tall, golden-skinned people with almond-shaped eyes. Extremely intelligent, agile, and willful. Natural affinity for magic.

⚔ Special: Naturally immune to paralyzation. Adept in all arcane arts.

Dark Elf — Province of Morrowind

Tall, dark-skinned people with red, glowing eyes. Extremely strong, intelligent, and quick. Innately blend magic with weapons.

⚔ Special: Receive (level ÷ 4) as a bonus to hit and damage with any melee or ranged weapon. Adept in blade, shield, and arcane arts.
