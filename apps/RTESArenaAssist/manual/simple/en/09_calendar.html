Dates & Calendar

A year in Tamriel has 360 days (12 months of 30 days each), and a week has 7 days. Years are written as "Era + year of that era". Example: 3E 389 = year 389 of the Third Era. Each era lasts about 1000 years.

This page is a quick reference for the calendar names that appear in dates. Use it to match a day or month name you see in game against its meaning.

Date format

Dates are written as "(weekday), (Nth) day of (month), (year)".

Example: Tirdas, 5th day of Hearthfire, 3E 389
= Tuesday, the 5th of Hearthfire (an autumn month), year 389 of the Third Era.
